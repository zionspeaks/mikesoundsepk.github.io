ABOUT MIKE SOUNDS

Born and raised in Brooklyn, DJ Mike Sounds began his career as a student of all music genres. While continuing his studies at Lehigh University in Bethlehem, Pa, Mike Sounds began hosting and DJ’ing parties on campus. With experience came opportunities to perform at local bars, lounges, and nightclubs where he quickly developed a following.

Mike Sounds has quickly established his name in the Lehigh Valley, earning residencies at the Revel Social, Corked Nightclub, Broadway Social, and other local bar/clubs. Mike Sounds served as the opener for Jai Wolf and Quinn Xcii at Stabler Arena. Most recently, he has branched out into major cities playing at Up & Down, Célon Lounge, Down Nightclub Philadelphia, and 5th & Mad. Now, Mike Sounds is based out of New York City working with major hospitality groups in the city.

Mike Sounds is well versed in all genres of music, including hip hop, house, edm, big room, rock, top 40, mashups, reggae, reggaeton, and classics. A true music lover, Mike Sounds has performed hundreds of open format sets in addition to creating relevant remixes, mashups, & original music with his collaborators. This constant production schedule provides a stronger connection to his following.
